Background

How I Built My Systems Thinking

01. Warehouse Operations Foundation

My journey began in warehouse operations management at Likha Digital Trading Inc. Working directly with teams and processes, I experienced firsthand the challenges of manual operations—tracking issues, communication gaps, and inefficient workflows that consumed valuable time.

02. Discovery of Google Sheets Automation

While managing daily operations, I started exploring Google Sheets beyond basic spreadsheets. I discovered that with the right structure and formulas, I could build systems that automatically tracked, calculated, and organized operational data—transforming chaos into clarity.

03. Scripting and System Design

As my systems grew more complex, basic formulas weren't enough. I learned Apps Script to create custom automations—automated alerts, data syncing, and report generation. This marked my transition from spreadsheet user to system builder.

04. Building Structured Dashboards

I began creating comprehensive dashboards that served as operational command centers. These systems provided real-time visibility into inventory, team performance, and workflow status—enabling data-driven decisions and proactive management.

05. HTML + Google Sheets Integration

To overcome the limitations of spreadsheet interfaces, I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I built custom frontend tools that connected to Google Sheets backends, creating proper user interfaces for team members to interact with operational data intuitively.

06. AI-Powered Automation

The latest evolution in my system building journey is integrating AI tools like Claude and ChatGPT. These enable intelligent automation—automated summaries, smart reporting, and decision support—pushing operational efficiency even further.
